# Calendar events not syncing

If your calendar events from Google Calendar, Exchange, or Outlook are not syncing to NotePlan, please make sure...

1. you have the native Calendar app installed (iOS and Mac),
2. you can see the events in the native Calendar app,
3. the calendar is added in the ["Internet Accounts"](28-how-to-add-new-calendars.md) preferences,
4. try to remove and add the calendar account again,
5. restart your complete device.
