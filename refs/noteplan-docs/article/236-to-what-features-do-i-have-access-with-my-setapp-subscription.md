# To what features do I have access with my Setapp subscription?

The Setapp subscription grants you access to nearly all App Store version features, including Encryption, Teamspaces, and the [web version](https://app.noteplan.co) (choose the Apple ID login).

There are a few differences compared to the App Store version:

- **No beta versions** — Beta versions are distributed exclusively via [TestFlight](https://testflight.apple.com/join/fm9q4OjE) for App Store subscribers. This means Setapp users have to wait longer for new features, bug fixes, and other improvements to arrive in the stable release.
- **Sharing of individual notes** is not available for Setapp users.
- **Lower AI credits** — Setapp users receive fewer monthly AI credits compared to App Store subscribers.
