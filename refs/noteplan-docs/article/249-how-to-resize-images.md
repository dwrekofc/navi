# How to resize images?

Images are displayed relative to the line width of the editor. To increase the line width of the editor, [see here](186-how-to-change-the-editor-text-margins-width.md).

**macOS
**In the settings > editor, you can adapt the image size.

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/678973a8b73b9761095d4a42/file-qPHLjSV6dR.png)**iOS**
Open the **settings** in the left sidebar (scroll to the bottom), then scroll to **"General"** in the settings and tap on **Font & Image Size**.

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/678973f1b73b9761095d4a43/file-RthhSvZafZ.png)
