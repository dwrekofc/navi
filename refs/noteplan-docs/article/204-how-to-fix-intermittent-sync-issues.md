# How to fix Intermittent Sync Issues

If sync works for you on some devices or it worked in the past but stopped now, please try to sign out with your Apple ID on your device and sign in again. You can do this by opening the system preferences, clicking on your Apple ID, and scrolling down for the "Sign Out..." button. This works on Mac and iOS:
