# Remove an external Calendar

Sometimes you have too many events synced from other calendars. You can remove some calendars and hide their events by opening the sync menu in the menubar on Mac: `Preferences -> Calendars`. Check off the calendars you don't wish to see in your notes.

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/6127caf012c07c18afdce760/file-z56dCHaf2l.png)

On iOS go to preferences (in the calendar or notes view see top left menu icon), then `Calendar >`.
