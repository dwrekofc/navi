# Digital Bullet Journal

NotePlan supports the different task states which are used in Bullet Journaling in a similar way:

- open task (* task),
- completed (* [x] task),
- scheduled (* [>] task)
- and canceled (* [-] task).
You can use shortcuts (CMD+D for completing, CMD+R for canceling, and Shift+CMD+D for scheduling) or click on the arrow left of the task when you hover over it to change the state.

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/61ec4e61d86136157d99c8c8/file-wSrYnYmtnE.gif)Use the daily notes for rapid logging just like the bullet journal does. Write tasks with an asterisk (* task) and bullets with a dash (- bullet). At the end of the day transfer or re-schedule open tasks to the next day.
In the regular notes, you can write your collections and lists.
