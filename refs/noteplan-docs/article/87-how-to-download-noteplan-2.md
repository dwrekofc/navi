# How to download NotePlan 2?

## Warning: NotePlan 2 is no longer supported. Purchase NotePlan 3 [here](https://noteplan.co).

We have removed NotePlan 2 from sale on the AppStore, so new customers can't purchase it, since NotePlan 2 is not supported anymore. You can still download your purchase from the AppStore, however.

## On Mac

If you have already purchased it and want to reinstall the app, you can still download it again from the AppStore by going to your recent purchases tab and click on the download button:

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/60ccdfc700fd0d7c253f7aaa/file-fLl63R52Dv.png)

If you have purchase NotePlan 2 on Mac from the website, using a license code, download it from here:

- [Download NotePlan 2 on Mac (website license)](https://noteplan.co/download)

## On iOS

Open the AppStore app, then tap on your profile picture top right to open your account. In your account page tap on "Purchased", then search for "NotePlan 2" and tap on the download button.

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/60cce23b8556b07a2884788e/file-1iWpnUZpRC.jpg)
