# Mac App Store download stuck

If the Mac App Store shows the circular download indicator for an app and then stops and returns to the **Get** / download button, it’s often caused by a stuck App Store session. Signing out and signing back in refreshes your purchase state and usually fixes it.

1. Open the **App Store** on your Mac.
2. In the macOS menu bar, click **Store** → **Sign Out**.
3. Quit the App Store completely (App Store → **Quit App Store**), then reopen it.
4. Search for the app again and click **Get** / the download button.
5. When prompted, sign in with your Apple ID and try the download again.

If it still doesn’t download, restart your Mac and repeat the steps above.
