# How to link a direct purchase (via Stripe) to my account?

When you purchase NotePlan directly using Stripe (instead of the App Store), you can link your subscription at the end of the purchase to NotePlan.

Make sure you have NotePlan installed on the device you are purchasing from and wait until the final confirmation page opens where you can click on "Activate".

You also receive an Email from us with the same instructions in case you accidentally close the page.

If the subscription is detected on one device, but not the other, you need to [sign in](195-sign-in-with-apple.md) in the "License"/"Subscription Details" settings.

**On macOS**
Open Settings (either through the menubar clicking on "NotePlan" > Settings or CMD+, then click on "License"), click on "Sign In".

**On iOS**
Open the left sidebar and tap on "Settings", then tap at the top on "Subscription Details", tap on "Sign In".

Note: The "Sign In" button is not available if you are using the TestFlight version, you need to download the App Store version, sign in, then use the TestFlight version.
