# Where do I submit Feedback (Features and Bugs)?

## Features

You can submit feature requests to your [feedback board](http://feedback.noteplan.co), where you can also search for existing feature requests and upvote them or contribute to the discussion.

## Improvement Suggestions

If you have something in between a feature and bug or just an idea how to improve something specific, also post it to our [feedback board](http://feedback.noteplan.co/) please.

## Bugs

You can submit bugs by reaching out directly to us, [[email protected]](cdn-cgi/l/email-protection.md). If it's not critical, you can also post it to our [Reddit Forum](http://reddit.com/r/noteplanapp/) or [join our Discord community](https://discord.gg/D4268MT) and share the bug in the #bugs channel.
