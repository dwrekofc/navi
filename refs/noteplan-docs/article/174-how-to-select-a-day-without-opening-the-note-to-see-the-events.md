# How to select a day without opening the note (to see the events)

When you are inside a regular (project) note, you might want to review some events of a specific day without having to open the note automatically.

You can do this (from v3.7.2) by shift-clicking (hold shift while clicking) on a day in the calendar (while you have a regular note opened). Then the day will be selected and the events will be shown without opening the daily note.

This works also with a right-click > "Show Events".
