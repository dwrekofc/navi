# Convert or format multiple lines of text

In NotePlan, you can easily convert a list of items into a checklist, task or other element by highlighting the entire list and then selecting the type you want to convert them into from the format menu. On Mac click on the "A" button at the top right or use the "Format" menubar. On iOS tap on the list icon in the toolbar:
