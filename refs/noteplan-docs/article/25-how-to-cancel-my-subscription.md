# How to cancel my subscription?

Depending on how you have subscribed (App Store, or directly through the website), you can take different paths to cancel your subscription.

## Web App

If you signed into the web app using [https://app.noteplan.co](https://app.noteplan.co/), a trial was started automatically without asking for your credit card. This trial will automatically cancel at the end. You don't need to do anything.

## Website

Open the [customer portal](https://billing.stripe.com/p/login/6oE6oU3lhbcv8gweUU) to manage your subscription. Or click on "Manage Subscription" under the Preferences > License from inside the native app or Settings > Manage Subscription from the web app.

## App Store

Since Apple handles all sales, cancellations, and refunds, we - the developer - have no access to your subscription.

You can cancel the subscription in the AppStore app. Click here to ** **[manage your subscriptions](itms-apps://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.md) (then select "NotePlan" and uncheck the subscription).

If you seem to have problems with your NotePlan 2 discount, [read this](article/68-how-does-the-noteplan-2-discount-work.md).

If it displays "Expires...", then your trial or subscription is canceled successfully
