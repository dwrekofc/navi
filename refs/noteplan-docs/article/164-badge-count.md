# Turn on/off badge count

The badge shows the number of open tasks for today. You can turn it on/off on Mac in the menubar under Window:

![ff371efaf4eed3f4ec341e8c6df55ae7.png](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/inline/124420/4e8c6353b1f77fd6d70372550293ec0fbf03147a/9e5fa53fdf1bb9f914212ae71dddcfa34ac8ad51/ff371efaf4eed3f4ec341e8c6df55ae7.png)
And on iOS you need to open the preferences app and look for NotePlan, then turn the badge off under "notifications". Then turn off "badges".

If this is not working for you click on this link: [noteplan://x-callback-url/iconBadge/?enable=no](noteplan://x-callback-url/iconBadge/)
