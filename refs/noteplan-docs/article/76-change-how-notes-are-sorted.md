# Change how notes are sorted

You can change how your notes are sorted in the menubar under "View" → "Sort By" on Mac and choose between the date edited, modified, or by title. This preference will be synced to your iOS devices.

## Mac

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/6098581381c9560e55febadb/file-g05iD8ybYv.png)

## iOS

Open the preferences and scroll down to the "General" section, you will find "Sort Notes By" at the bottom of that section:

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/645cf173213fbb442001df17/file-LbqCBVHxLs.png)

### Pro-Tips

- If you choose "title", you can prepend the folder and note names with numbers to control the order.

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/609858990f9ba0314b0ad7db/file-oT7T7eXZs5.png)

- Deselect "Sort Folders Above Notes", if you don't want that the folders are always sorted to the top. Then you can prepend a note with "!" or "0" to get it to the top of the sidebar, like "!Inbox" for example.
