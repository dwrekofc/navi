# How to refund my subscription?

## Website

If you have subscribed to a plan through the website, reach out to us by sending us an email to [[email protected]](cdn-cgi/l/email-protection.md), and share your name, or email address you have used during the subscription process. We give refunds for up to 14 days after the subscription started.

## App Store

If you purchased through the App Store, Apple handles all sales, cancellations, and refunds, we - the developer - have no access to your purchase, subscription, or other customer data. For refunds, you need to submit a request to Apple.

You can refund your subscription (or other purchases) from the purchase confirmation email you have received by clicking on "Report a problem" or read [this help article by Apple](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204084).
