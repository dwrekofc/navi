# How to see widgets on macOS

We have introduced widget on the iOS versions in v3.9.8 and you can sync the widgets from iOS to your Mac as well. Here are the requirements:

- iOS 17, with macOS 14 and NotePlan 3.9.8 is installed.
- You have added a NotePlan widget on your iOS device.
- The iOS device is on the same wifi network as your Mac.
- You are signed into the same Apple ID on macOS and iOS.
- Your iOS device is near your macOS device (if you are not on the same wifi, means bluetooth needs to be enabled).

If this is all fulfilled, you should be able to see NotePlan widgets on macOS.

For more information on how to add widgets on macOS Sonoma or newer, visit: [https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT213659](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT213659)
