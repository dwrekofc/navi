# What do the colors in the calendar mean?

The iOS version displays colored dots in the calendar above the note.

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/63f887fdc490cd5d5b96a42a/file-BwstUQMxTR.png)

Each colored dot represents something in NotePlan:

- Purple dot: Day contains at least one event
- Green check: Day contains at least one completed task
- Orange circle: Day contains at least one open task
- Red background: Day contains overdue tasks
