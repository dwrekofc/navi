# How to create sample/demo templates?

There are two types of sample templates: 1. Regular/Calendar Note templates and 2. Meeting Note templates.

### 1. Regular/Calendar Note Templates

Right click (macOS) or long-press (iOS) "Templates" in the sidebar > "Create sample templates":

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/6780409638a01c46d679c7a6/file-nyGJhqVOYH.png)

### 2. Meeting Note Templates

When you attempt to create a Meeting Note, you will find a button "Create sample templates" at the bottom in the menus on iOS and macOS.

**macOS**: You create Meeting Notes by clicking the event in the right sidebar, then "Create New Note".
**iOS:** Long-press the event at the top or tap on the event in the timeline (at the bottom) to open the menu, then tap on "Create New Note".
