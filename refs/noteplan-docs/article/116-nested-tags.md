# Nested Tags

## Find anything with Nested Tags and Global Search

Structure your notes and tasks into a folder-like structure using tags. Combined with a search spanning your calendar and project notes you don't have to worry about where to keep everything

You can use `#tags` for things and `@tags` for people to tag a specific paragraph (or task). To nest your tags, add a slash between parent and child tags, for example, `#books/business` or `@team/jakob`.

Use nested tagging to reference your team easily or specific information about a project with `#noteplan/marketing/analytics`. Or build your CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system out of nested tags with `@jakob/phone`, `@jakob/address`, etc.
