# Multi-Day Timeline

You can now see up to 7 days on your timeline with all events, reminders, and tasks. The multi-day view lets you look ahead and plan better. Use the settings icon top right of the calendar to change what should be displayed in the sidebar.

## How it works

Open the Daily Notes and click on the settings button on the top right of your calendar (on Mac) to configure how many days you want to see in the timeline:

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/645a780d1140db1f380b7cb2/file-U7eGi1E0cB.png)

"Timeline" is already preselected if you open a Daily Note (such as today's note). This option will always show the day you have selected in the calendar on the left side as the first day:

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/645a788daea82e179c63b50c/file-4YMjfLERCD.png)

If you select another day, the timeline will update and show the new day on the left.

### Fixed Week Timeline

You have an alternative option to show a timeline with a "fixed week". This means you can select any day in the week, the timeline will always begin with the first day of the week. This is also the default when you open a Weekly Note and configure the sidebar to show a timeline instead of a list.

### List View

A third option is to show a list of events and reminders instead of a timeline, which shows always the event of this week if you are inside a Daily or Weekly Note:

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/645a795051152c21f5041a08/file-3aVCfAOb2a.png)
