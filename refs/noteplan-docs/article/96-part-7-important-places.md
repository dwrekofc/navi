# Part 7: Important Places

😌 We have reached the end of this email course, but for you, it's just the beginning!

Thank you for taking the time to learn about NotePlan. In this email I want to share a few important places which will help you continue your productivity journey:

- **Discord Community: ** [Get Invited](https://discord.gg/D4268MT)
- Stay up to date with beta versions.
- Chat with other users in real-time, ask questions, share things.
- Receive important announcements.
- **Reddit Community: ** [Join](https://www.reddit.com/r/noteplanapp)
- Ask the community, start a discussion. Find new themes.
- **X:** [Follow](https://x.com/noteplanapp)
- Stay up to date about new versions and the development progress.
- **Stacey's Walkthrough Videos:** [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DCecVFgTl14&list=PLygLI_TgqLMlORsM3xbbWKVLqBVFxlf4A)
- Recap everything you learned so far by watching Stacey's series.
- **Inside Look Videos (real-life user workflows)**
- [Colin's Daily Workflow](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VUERpdWoPso) (weekly and monthly reviews with templates, "Big 3" prioritization, Apple Shortcuts and the amazing "Dashboard" plugin)
- [George's (CMO of Verge.io) Project Management Workflow](https://youtu.be/J-FlyffE9iA) (task capturing, Shortcuts, Timeblocking, Publish Notes)
- **Complete NotePlan on iPad walkthrough: **[Watch](https://youtu.be/xPB_25YbZJ4?si=uLZk8NLEf8pu6TiK)
- A beginner-friendly introduction to NotePlan on iPad.
- **Custom Themes:** [Read](44-customize-themes.md)
- Create custom themes if you want different colors and fonts than available in the system themes.
- You can also use custom regular expressions to add more Markdown styles.
- We have a GitHub repo with a ton of user themes [here](https://github.com/brokosz/NotePlan_Themes).
- **Plugins:** [Read](65-commandbar-plugins.md)
- Watch Stacey's introduction to plugins [here](https://youtu.be/jZYtU1qTE-w).
- Find a list of plugins inside the app in Settings > Plugins or search [here](http://noteplan.co/plugins).

**It's your turn**

Build your productivity system and keep tweaking it. And share your experience with the rest of us.
