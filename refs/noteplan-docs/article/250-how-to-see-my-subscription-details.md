# How to see my subscription details?

To see the details of your current subscription, you need to navigate to the settings of NotePlan on iOS or macOS.

**macOS
**Open the settings (CMD+, or see "NotePlan" > "Settings..." in the menubar), then click on "License".

**![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/678e5648ca2c924d66e98ccd/file-qho0cqjBRw.png)**

**iOS**
In the left sidebar scroll to the bottom and tap on "Settings". At the top of your settings you should see your subscription status, to see more details, tap on "Subscription Details". Here you can also tap on "Manage Subscription" to cancel your subscription.
