# Support for Exchange, Outlook, CalDAV, and other Calendars

NotePlan supports all calendars that can be added through your [macOS and iOS "Internet Accounts" system preferences](28-how-to-add-new-calendars.md), such as Microsoft Exchange, Google, Yahoo, Aol.

## Outlook

Outlook is not directly listed, but you can use "Microsoft Exchange" instead to link your Outlook calendar. Another option is to [share your calendar](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/sync-your-icloud-calendar-with-outlook-for-mac-c9c67e41-274f-4527-ae5e-ea1003d89fc5) with Apple Calendar, which will pull the events you create in Outlook and also display in NotePlan

## CalDAV

If your service is not listed, you can also add calendars through "CalDAV". Click on "Add Other Account..." to reveal this option:

## Possible Problems with Exchange

If your Exchange events are not appearing in NotePlan, you might need to complete the MFA (multi-factor authentication) with your organization's systems. Reach out to your IT department or search your intranet's help articles to clarify the process ( [here's an example](https://harvard.service-now.com/ithelp?id=kb_article&sys_id=95cbdc4edbe55bc0d1257ee5bf961935)).
