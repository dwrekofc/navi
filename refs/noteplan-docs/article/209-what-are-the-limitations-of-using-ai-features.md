# What are the limitations of using AI features?

We are using OpenAI to create the responses or to transcribe the audio and every response incurs a small cost. Therefore we are limiting the maximum responses per month. This number can change depending on the pricing set by OpenAI, the model and how much AI features are used in general.

If you reach the maximum (i.e. are a heavy AI user), you can use your private [OpenAI account](207-set-personal-openai-api-key.md).
