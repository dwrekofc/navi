# How to activate my Setapp subscription on iOS?

**Note:** You need to be subscribed to [Setapp’s iOS plan](https://support.setapp.com/hc/en-us/articles/360010453859). Read [here](https://support.setapp.com/hc/en-us/articles/360017865419) how to add devices to your plan.

On macOS (install the Setapp app), click on the Setapp symbol in the menu bar, then "Open Setapp". Search and select "NotePlan". Right to the "Open" button, click on "iOS app" and follow the instructions.

![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/608352f7e0324b5fdfd0e50f/file-aCkn0MCCCo.png)

If you run into problems, open your account settings at [https://my.setapp.com/subscription](https://my.setapp.com/subscription) and click on "Manage Devices". Remove your mobile devices and try to activate the iOS app again.

You can also activate the iOS version of NotePlan from a web browser using your Setapp account page. For more details see [Setapp's support pages](https://support.setapp.com/hc/en-us/articles/360015976820-Install-and-activate-iOS-apps).
