# I can't renew my subscription

If you are trying to renew your subscription inside NotePlan and it's not working, you can try it through the AppStore app: [itms-apps://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions](itms-apps://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.md) (copy this link into Safari if it's not clickable).

Then click on "Edit" beside NotePlan and choose a plan here:
![](../../d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/6081f7f4c9133261f23f4b41/images/61e1a256d6e0de41c5d476d3/file-9MBLHmW5dG.png)
If this is not working for you, please send us screenshots and more details of where you are trying to renew and about the error you are receiving. You can also [send us a log](57-how-to-enable-logging.md) of your failed attempt.
